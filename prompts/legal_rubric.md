# Legal Hallucination Rubric (v0.1)

## Categories
1. Fabricated legal authority (fake statute/case/citation).
2. Unsupported obligations or rights not present in source text.
3. Internal contradiction to provided constraints.
4. Jurisdiction mismatch.

## Reviewer Scores (1-5)
- Legal accuracy
- Faithfulness to source text
- Draft usability
- Risk level (Low/Medium/High)
